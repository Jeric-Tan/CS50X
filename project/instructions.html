How to Play

Objective

The objective of the game is achieve 1 pair and 4 sets. A pair is two identical tiles. A set can either three identical tiles or three consecutive numbers in the same suit. A single tile cannot be used in two sets at once.

Play

When the game starts, you are given 14 random tiles. Each turn a random tile would be drawn and players would need to decide if they want to keep the newly drawn tile or discard the drawn tile. If they decide to keep the newly drawn tile, the tile would replace the discarded tile chosen by the player. While if they decide to discard the drawn tile, there would be no changes to the hand and the next turn begins. At the end, the player's score would be the number of turns they took to win the game.
